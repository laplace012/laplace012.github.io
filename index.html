About Ruishen Zhao

My name is Ruishen Zhao. During 2022.09-2025.06 I was a postdoc in Morningside Center of Mathematics. Since 2025.09 I became a postdoc in Yau Mathematical Sciences Center.

Wir müssen wissen. Because it's there.

Research & Interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests lie in p-adic automorphic forms, eigenvarieties, and Shimura varieties. A central theme of my work is the arithmetic of motives, especially their p-adic variation.
