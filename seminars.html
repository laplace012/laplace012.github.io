Seminars & Talks

I always enjoy discussing mathematics and participating in seminars. Below is a (necessarily) incomplete list of my activities.

2025 Autumn Seminar on Pan Lue's Work Venue: YMSC
Date: 2025 Autumn Semester

This seminar is organized by Hansheng Diao and me. We mainly study Pan Lue's work "On locally analytic vectors of the completed cohomology of modular curves" (Pan2022).

2025 Fall Seminar on Igusa Stacks Venue: BICMR
Date: 2025 Fall Semester

This seminar is organized by Liang Xiao. We mainly study the recent development of Igusa Stacks, "A PEL-type Igusa Stack and the p-adic Geometry of Shimura Varieties" (Zhang2023), and "Igusa stacks and the cohomology of Shimura varieties" (DvHKZ2024).

Seminar on geometry of Shimura varieties Venue: BICMR
Date: 2025 Spring

This seminar is organized Liang Xiao. We mainly study some recent development of Shimura varieties and focus on "Cycles on Shimura varieties via geometric Satake" (XZ2017), "Geometric Satake, categorical traces, and arithmetic of Shimura varieties" (Zhu2018), and "Coherent sheaves on the stack of Langlands parameters" (Zhu2020).

p-adic Langlands Program seminar Venue: MCM
Date: Since 2024 September

This is a series seminar organized by MCM. See "p-adic Langlands Program Seminar" (MCM).

p-adic Geometry Learning Seminar Venue: MCM
Date: Since 2022 September

This is a series seminar organized by MCM and YMSC. See "p-adic Geometry Learning Seminar" (MCM-YMSC).
